What is Marvel?

The Marvel Universe is a fictional shared universe where the stories of Marvel Comics take place..

It features superheroes, villains, aliens, gods, mutants, and other beings that inhabit Earth and various other planets and dimensions. The Marvel Universe is constantly expanding and evolving, as new characters are created and old ones are reimagined. Some of the most iconic and popular characters of the Marvel Universe are Spider-Man, Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and the X-Men..
