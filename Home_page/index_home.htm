About:-

The Marvel Universe is a fictional shared universe where the stories of Marvel Comics take place. It is inhabited by superheroes, villains, aliens, gods, mutants, and other fantastic beings. The Marvel Universe has a rich history that spans over 80 years of publication, and has been adapted into various media, such as movies, TV shows, video games, and more. Some of the most iconic characters of the Marvel Universe are Spider-Man, Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and the X-Men.
